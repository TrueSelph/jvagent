# Code of Conduct

This project follows the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**.

The full text is published at:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating in this project — in issues, pull requests, discussions, and
any other community space — you agree to abide by its terms. In short: be
respectful, welcoming, and constructive; harassment and exclusionary behavior
are not tolerated.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers at
**admin@trueselph.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly, and reporter confidentiality will be respected.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take appropriate
corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, as
described in the Contributor Covenant's Enforcement Guidelines (linked above).
