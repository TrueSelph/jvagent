# Proposal: Mobile Commerce Platform

**Client:** RetailMart Inc.
**Date:** January 2025
**Prepared by:** V75 Inc.

---

## 1. Executive Summary

RetailMart requires a cross-platform mobile application to extend its e-commerce presence into mobile, competing with emerging direct-to-consumer brands. We propose building a React Native application with a headless commerce backend, delivered in two phases over 5 months. The solution will support iOS and Android from a single codebase, integrate with RetailMart's existing inventory management system, and provide a personalized shopping experience driven by customer data.

**Investment Range:** $180,000 - $220,000

---

## 2. Understanding of Your Needs

### Background

RetailMart operates 45 brick-and-mortar stores across the Midwest and launched its e-commerce platform in 2022. Mobile traffic now accounts for 60% of web visits, but the current responsive website provides a suboptimal mobile experience.

### Key Objectives

- Launch iOS and Android apps with feature parity
- Integrate with existing inventory and order management systems
- Support buy-online-pick-up-in-store (BOPIS) workflow
- Achieve 4.5+ star app store rating within 90 days of launch

### Success Criteria

- 15% of e-commerce revenue from mobile app within 6 months
- App crash-free rate above 99.5%
- Checkout completion rate above 80%

---

## 3. Our Approach

### Methodology

We will apply a phased agile approach, delivering a Minimum Loveable Product (MLP) in Phase 1 and adding advanced features in Phase 2.

### Why This Approach

Cross-platform development with React Native allows simultaneous iOS and Android delivery while maintaining native performance. Our headless commerce architecture ensures the app remains independent of backend changes.

### Risks and Mitigations

- **Inventory sync latency:** Implement a thin caching layer with 30-second TTL
- **Payment integration complexity:** Use Stripe's proven mobile SDK

---

## 4. Scope of Work

### Phase 1: Core Experience (Months 1-3)
- Product browsing with search and filters
- User accounts and authentication
- Shopping cart and checkout
- Order history and tracking
- BOPIS integration

### Phase 2: Engagement (Months 4-5)
- Personalized recommendations
- Push notifications
- Loyalty program integration
- Analytics and dashboards

### Out of Scope
- Native Android/iOS-specific features (camera, AR)
- Internationalization
