# Research Report: V75 Inc. and Eldon Marks

## V75 Inc.

**Overview:**
V75 Inc. is a technology company based in Georgetown, Guyana, specializing in software development, conversational AI, and digital transformation. The company is known for its team of certified conversational AI engineers, software developers, and innovators. V75 Inc. focuses on automating and optimizing business process operations for clients across various sectors, including banking, sales, and customer service. ([V75 Inc. Official Site](https://v75inc.com/), [ZoomInfo](https://www.zoominfo.com/c/v75-inc/472532737))

**Founders and Leadership:**
- **Eldon Marks** – Founding Director and CEO
- **Jason Mars** – Director
- **Christopher Clarke** – Director, AI Researcher
- **Asa Brouet** – Director, Chief Technology Officer ([V75 Inc. Team](https://v75inc.com/our-team))

**Key Projects and Achievements:**
- Exclusive digital partner of the Guyana Energy Conference and Supply Chain Expo, specializing in the development of AI products for the event ([Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/GuyanaEnergyConference/posts/v75-inc-is-the-exclusive-digital-partner-of-the-guyana-energy-conference-and-sup/642356291512581/)).
- Launched the NeXus Hub Initiative, a project aimed at fostering an environment of innovation and supporting local tech talent ([V75 Inc. Stories](https://v75inc.com/stories)).
- Developed productivity and automation software applications, including tools to improve workplace productivity and automate organizational reporting ([V75 Inc. Productivity](https://v75inc.com/posts/tag:productivity)).
- Committed to creating local employment and training opportunities for Guyanese tech professionals ([Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/newsroomgy/posts/v75-inc-a-local-technology-company-has-teamed-up-with-professor-jason-mars-creat/3677414432535563/)).

## Eldon Marks

**Background:**
Eldon Marks is a Guyanese tech entrepreneur, academic, and software developer with over two decades of experience in the technology sector. He is recognized for his work as a mentor, educator, and serial entrepreneur, having founded multiple organizations including V75 Inc. and NeXus Hub Inc. ([eldonmarks.com](https://eldonmarks.com/), [LinkedIn](https://gy.linkedin.com/in/eldonmarks))

**Career Highlights:**
- Founder and CEO of V75 Inc. (since 2019)
- Founder and Director of NeXus Hub Inc. (since 2018)
- Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Guyana ([UG Profile](https://fns.ug.edu.gy/staff/mr-eldon-marks))
- Advocate for retaining and nurturing local tech talent in Guyana ([Guyana Energy Conference](https://guyanaenergy.gy/post?slug=guyanese-tech-leader-says-retaining-local-talent-key-to-fueling-nations-digital-transformation))
- Personal mission to create empowering environments for innovation and tech industry development in Guyana ([IIC Profile](https://www.iicom.org/profile/eldon-marks/))

**Personal Background:**
- Grew up in South Ruimveldt Garden, Guyana
- Known for mentoring thousands of students and young professionals in the tech field ([Kaieteur News](https://kaieteurnewsonline.com/2018/09/30/entrepreneur-software-developer-and-educator-eldon-marks-is-a-special-person/))
